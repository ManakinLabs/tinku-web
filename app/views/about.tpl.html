What is Tinku?

If you’re concerned about the well being of those in need around you, giving them a hand should be easy. Today, “giving a hand” is frequently executed through volunteering, which can be a delicate process. Finding an NGO that fits your vision for impact is time-consuming, and many of the activities that volunteering implies may be tedious. This often leads potential helpers to face a dilemma: should they invest their free time “for others” by volunteering, or “for themselves” by doing the activities they love the most with their close ones? If you’re more often inclined to the second option, we don’t blame you – we do the same. But what if you could do both at the same time?
